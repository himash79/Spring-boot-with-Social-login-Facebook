01) Goto `https://developers.facebook.com`
02) Login with facebook account
03) Choose Getting start / Already signin click My-Apps
04) Fill the details and continue
05) Click Create App
06) Choose app purpose and continue
07) Fill the Provide basic information
08) Select Settings
09) Choose Basic and get copy of `App ID` and `App Secret`
10) Done

* After sign in as facebook account through the project who can see sign in details as below,
	01) When developer sign in after get notification from facebook account.
	    Or	
	02) Login facebook social account --> Settings --> Apps & Websites --> Developer App name
